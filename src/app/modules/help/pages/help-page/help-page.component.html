Introduction

Reservation system allows you to create your own virtual rooms for H.323/SIP videoconferences or Adobe Connect web conferences. You can share the rooms with your colleagues and use them for your conference calls. The creation of new rooms is allowed only for logged in users with certain level of authenticity. You can log in with you user account from your organization that is involved in eduID.cz federation or you can create a new user account at hostel.

Terms and conditions under which personal data collated in providing the CESNET e-infrastructure services are processed you can find here.

You can create a new room from main page or from page with your reservation requests.

Level of authenticity

Only logged in user with a certain level of authorization can create a new room or book a capacity for existing room. If you see warning next to your name on the top of the page, it means that your account doesn't meet the required level of authorization. In this case you have two options:

  • Request your identity provider to verify your identity (e.g., if you use hostel identity, you can follow this procedure).
  • Request the system administrators to grant you the permissions to create new rooms and book room capacities.

Rooms

Virtual rooms can be used for multiple conference calls (e.g., periodic meetings). The room can be created for longer period of time (a number of years), but throughout the period it isn't automatically available for participants to join. For each conference call in the virtual room you must book capacity, which determines how many participants will be able to join to the room. The virtual room gets assigned identifiers on creation which don't change throughout the room's existence (if you don't requet a change). Thus the identifiers are the same for all conference calls that take place in the virtual room.

You can imagine the virtual room as a room with a table, but without chairs. Therefore participants cannot just come and sit down to take a meeting, but someone has to „prepare chairs” in advance for every meeting.

Once you prepare chairs in the virtual room (i.e., on room creation or when you book requested capacity), participants can enter the room, sit down and the meeting can begin. For each meeting you can prepare different number of chairs. The room is situated in the same building on the same location and it's invariably labeled (e.g., My room) and therefore all meetings take place at the same location.

Resources

Reservation system manages hardware and software equipment (referred to as resources), which provides the creation and management of virtual rooms. Each resource has only a limited number of licenses that can be used at a time. For instance if you want to join an Adobe Connect room, the resource must have at least one available license, otherwise it won't allow you to join the room. After joining the room the license is consumed and after leaving the room the license is released. To be able to guarantee a specific number of participants to be able to join to every room, it is necessery to book the licenses in advance. That is the reason why you must explicitly book a requested capacity for each room when you want to use it (when booking a capacity for virtual room).

You can imagine the resource as a building in which few rooms are located and the limited number of licenses you can imagine as chairs located in this building and which may be moved between the rooms. Each room can contain different number of chairs at different times. For every meeting in every room, the chairs must be booked in advance, otherwise it may happen that participants won't be able to sit down.
